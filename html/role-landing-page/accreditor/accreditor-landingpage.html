MESSAGE FROM THE DIRECTOR

Quality assurance through the process of accreditation knows no boundaries. It is a never-ending process of improvement. Accreditation is a process by which an institution evaluates its educational activities and seeks an independent judgment to confirm the quality and continuous improvement of its programs.

Today, to ensure that we provide our students and stakeholders the best quality of service and education, PUP Taguig voluntarily submits five (5) programs — BSBA (Bachelor of Science in Business Administration: Marketing, Human Resources), BS Office Administration (Bachelor of Science in Office Administration), BS Accountancy (Bachelor of Science in Accountancy), BSIT (Bachelor of Science in Information Technology), and BSED (Bachelor of Secondary Education) — to the accreditation process in 2025. We remain committed to continuous improvement, transparency, and academic excellence as we work together to strengthen our programs.

We are honored to be visited by seasoned accreditors and educational leaders who will help ensure the best educational experience for future generations. On behalf of the University officials, I extend my heartfelt gratitude for your expertise and time.
